MARKETING TAB — expected server routes (any one per group is sufficient)

KPIs & Timeseries
  • GET /api/ads/google/kpis?from&to
  • GET /api/ads/google/stats?from&to
  • GET /api/marketing/kpis?from&to
  • GET /api/marketing/stats?from&to
  • GET /api/ga4/kpis?from&to
  • GET /api/ga4/timeseries?from&to

Campaigns
  • GET  /api/ads/google/campaigns?q=
  • GET  /api/ads/linkedin/campaigns?q=
  • GET  /api/ads/x/campaigns?q=
  • POST /api/ads/google/campaigns              {name,channel,dailyBudget}
  • POST /api/ads/google/campaigns/:id/status   {status:"active"|"paused"}
  • (Generic alt) GET/POST /api/marketing/campaigns, /api/marketing/campaigns/:id/status

Audiences & GA
  • POST /api/ads/google/audience/sync           {segmentId}
  • POST /api/ga4/events                         [{name,params}]

Budgets
  • GET/POST /api/marketing/budgets              [{channel,percent}]

AUTH: Bearer token via Authorization header (UI reads localStorage.apiToken)
